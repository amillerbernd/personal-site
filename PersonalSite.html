About me

Hi there! I'm Annie. I write, report, analyze and visualize data, craft and execute content strategy and design things. All of that is pretty exclusive to the internet and in no particular order. Right now, I contract with USAA as a content editor and with the Rivard Report as a data journalist.

Previously, I helped the Express-News build out its digital strategy, coordinated training for reporters and did some data reporting. Before that, I covered breaking news in California.

I live and eat tacos in San Antonio, Texas with my boyfriend and our Chihuahua, who thinks she's a German Shepherd.
